About The Association

Who We Are

Founded in 2015, DMV Ekimogun Association is a registered community organization representing Ondo State indigenes living in the DMV region of the United States.

Our Purpose

To promote unity, cultural heritage, community development, social welfare, and positive representation of Ekimogun people abroad.

Our Values

Ìṣọ̀kan, ìbáṣepọ̀, ìtẹ́lọ́run àwùjọ — Unity, cooperation, and community service guide everything we do.
